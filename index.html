Creative Design Competition

Do you have creativity? We are looking for the next most unique glass cup design! Participate in our creative design competition, showcase your ideas, and have the opportunity to turn your designs into real products!

Glass Craft Experience Day

Bring your family and friends to join our Glass Craft Experience Day! In this exciting event, you will have the opportunity to make your own glass by hand. Our professional craftsmen will personally guide you to experience the fun and challenges of glass craftsmanship.

Timeline

This module is used to introduce the development history of enterprises

  • 2016-2020

    Building a brand and expanding the market

    During this period, the company has been committed to establishing its own brand image and market position. Through continuous research and innovation, the company has launched a series of uniquely designed and high-quality glass cup products to meet the growing needs of consumers.

  • 2020-2023

    Transforming environmental protection and improving efficiency

    As consumer demand for environmentally friendly products continues to increase, the company will shift its focus to sustainable development and environmental protection concepts. The company has started researching and producing environmentally friendly glass cups made from recycled glass materials, and gradually phased out the traditional disposable plastic cup product line.

  • 2024

    Leading environmental protection and intelligent production

    In 2024, the company has become one of the leading enterprises in the field of environmentally friendly glass cups. The company continuously innovates and launches more product lines that meet market demand, including customized services and personalized designs, to meet the needs of different customer groups.
